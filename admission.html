NAME OF COURSE ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
B.PHARM

Candidate must have passed 10+2 examination conducted by the respective state/central government authorities recognized as equivalent to 10+2 examination by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) with English as one of the subjects and Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (P.C.M) and or Biology (P.C.B / P.C.M.B.) as optional subjects individually.

Candidate must have attained the age of 17 years as on 31.12.2024

B.PHARM (LATERAL ENTRY)

Passed Diploma in Pharmacy from a PCI approved Institution under section 12 of the Pharmacy Act 1948.

Candidate must have attained the age of 19 years as on 31.12.2024

D. PHARM

Candidate must have passed 10+2 examination with PCB / PCM by the respective state/central government educational boards recognized as equivalent to 10+2 examination.

Selection Mode – Candidates must have to appear for the state level entrance examination JEECUP, which is required for admission to diploma courses in Uttar Pradesh including Diploma in Pharmacy.

Candidate must have attained the age of 17 years as on 31-12-2024.
