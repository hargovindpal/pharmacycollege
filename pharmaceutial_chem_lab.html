PHARMACEUTIAL CHEMISTRY LAB

The Modern College of Pharmacy is having a well-established Machine room containing various machines required for various Unit Operations simulating with Pharmaceutical Industry Unit operations. Machine room is designed to acquire skills in learning the manufacture of various solid, liquid and semi-solid formulations and machines are also available for evaluation of several pharmaceutical dosage forms. The machine room equipped with most advanced instruments and machinery used in manufacturing of tablets, capsules, injectables, liquid orals and ointment sections. The Machine room is provided with stabilized electric power supply system. Each machine has separate platform with Standard Operating Procedure, Log Book, Standard Cleaning Procedure and Calibration or validation and operation manual. This laboratory provides a window for young and aspiring pharmacist to be well educated with the manufacturing aspect of different formulation in real time.

Pharmaceutical Chemistry Laboratory-I:

These well furnished laboratories provide facilities for practical training in pharmaceutical medicinal chemistry, synthesis and structural elucidation of medicinally important compounds, practical training is also offered in organic chemistry with well designed experiments. Students familiarized with various organic compounds by their nature, physical and chemical properties and usage in chemical reactions as well as in pharmaceutical industries in this laboratory.

Pharmaceutical Chemistry Laboratory-II:

In this laboratory the experiments based on qualitative and quantitative determination of impurities present in various inorganic compounds. The experiments aimed separation and identification of inorganic and organic mixture are also carried out in this lab. The pharmaceutical chemistry lab-II is well ventilated, exhausted and equipped with various instruments such as magnetic stirrers, refractometer, Deionisation unit, infrared moisture balance, polarimeter, Photo fluorimeter, digital photo colorimeter, distillation unit and hot air oven.

Pharmaceutical Analysis Laboratory:

This lab is equipped with glassware for pharmaceutical analysis like burette, pipette, volumetric flasks, measuring cylinders, micro pipette etc. This laboratory helps students accounted with the modern methods of analysis and Good Laboratory Practice (GLPs) etc. This lab played a significant role in the qualitative and quantitative analysis of drugs in accordance with various pharmacopoeias methods.
