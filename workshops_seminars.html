Plantation drive programme

On 22th July, 2023 Plantation drive programme was organised. The duel objectives of this drive were to enhance the tree cover on the campus and to sensitize the students towards importance of trees.

National Pharmacovigilance Week

On the 3rd National Pharmacovigilance Week two days activities were organised on 22th & 23rd September 2023 under the theme “Promoting Public Trust in Pharmacovigilance" in which pesentation by Mr. AvnishPateria, faculty of Pharmacy department was given followed by Poster Presentation Competition. Pharmacovigilance is the science and activities related to detecting, evaluating, understanding and preventing adverse effects or problems related to any drug/vaccine. The objective of this two-day program was to create awareness among the students, which was organized successfully.

World Pharmacists Day

On the occasion of World Pharmacists Day on *25th September 2023* (Monday), Free Eye Checkup Camp in collaboration with Sharp Sight Eye Hospital, Jhansi was organised. The camp started at 10:00 am and continued till 03:00 pm. In the presence of ophthalmologist and optometrist, eyes of students of B.Pharm and D.Pharm along with all the pharmacy staff members were examined. This program not only helped the students in protecting their eyesight from damage but all the students being students of Modern College of Pharmacy in future will also be given the facility to get one time free eye check up for their family members. The event was fruitful as many students benefited from free eye check-up.

National current Good Manufacturing Practices Day

On the occasion of National current Good Manufacturing Practices Day on 10th Oct. 2023 (Tuesday) students were made aware about the importance of a Safe and effective manufacturing processes used in Pharmaceutical Industries through a seminar presented by Mr. ShubhamPachori, faculty of Pharmacy department.

Apart from this, today World Mental Health Day was also celebrated with the theme "Mental Health is a Universal Human Right”.
